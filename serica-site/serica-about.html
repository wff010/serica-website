Our Story

The Silk Legacy

Where Skin Meets Silk

The name Serica has been with us from the beginning. A reminder of what we're working toward with every treatment, every consultation, every client.

Photo coming soon

Three thousand years ago, the ancient world knew the far east as Serica: the lands of silk. Strand by strand, the silk masters of old combed through each fiber to create something flawless, smooth, and luminous. The skin is not so different. A living fabric of collagen and elastin capable of extraordinary things when treated with the right combination of science and care.

What began in Flushing serving New York's Chinese and Korean communities has grown into a two-location practice rooted equally in the ancient healing arts of East Asia and the most advanced clinical aesthetic technology available. Thousands of years of philosophy, technique, and understanding of the human body inform how we approach skin today, infused into everything we do alongside modern science.

How We Work

Our Philosophy

01
Clinical Precision

Every device we use is FDA-cleared and every claim on our menu is verified against manufacturer data. We operate under physician supervision. Your safety is not a disclaimer. It's the foundation of everything we do.

02
Eastern Wisdom

Serica was shaped by practices that predate modern medicine by millennia. Meridian bodywork, the philosophy that skin reflects the whole body, the precision of Traditional Chinese Medicine. These are not wellness trends. They are deep, evolved systems of care that we take seriously and carry forward.

03
Personal Connection

Every plan is built around a specific person and every follow-up measures real progress. Each client receives personalized treatment recommendations based on precise data.

Legal & Clinical Standards

Serica Skincare & MedSpa operates in full compliance with New York State medical and aesthetic regulations. The following disclosures apply to all treatments performed at both locations.

Physician Supervision
Medical Director
JV

Dr. John Ventrudo, MD

Medical Director · Serica Skincare & MedSpa

Dr. Ventrudo is a New York State licensed physician specializing in interventional pain management and physical medicine. He earned his Doctor of Medicine from the American University of the Caribbean School of Medicine and completed his residency in Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation at Montefiore Medical Center, and a neuromuscular and pain medicine fellowship at New York University Medical Center.

Dr. Ventrudo provides physician supervision and medical oversight for all aesthetic treatments performed at Serica. His role ensures all procedures follow appropriate medical protocols and New York State regulations. He previously served as Medical Director at SUNY Maritime College and as team physician for varsity athletic programs, and is a retired Commissioned Lieutenant in the United States Naval Reserve.

Clinical Standards & Treatment Information
Licensed Practitioners & Medical Disclaimer

All treatments are performed by licensed professionals under physician supervision in accordance with New York State medical guidelines. Some procedures may be considered medical treatments under New York State law. The following applies:

  • All medical-adjacent procedures are performed by licensed professionals under physician supervision in accordance with New York State regulations.
  • Treatment plans are determined following individual consultation and skin assessment. No treatment is administered without prior client consent.
  • Individual results vary. Outcomes depend on individual skin type, age, lifestyle, and adherence to post-treatment protocols.
  • All device-based treatments offered at Serica are FDA-cleared for their indicated uses. Off-label applications, where applicable, are discussed fully during consultation.
  • Serica does not diagnose, treat, or cure any medical condition. Clients with active medical conditions are advised to consult their physician prior to booking.
